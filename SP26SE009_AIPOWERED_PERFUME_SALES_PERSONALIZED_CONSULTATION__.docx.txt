(*) 3.1. Capstone Project name: 
* English: AI-Powered Perfume Sales & Personalized Consultation System
* Vietnamese: Hệ thống quản lý bán nước hoa và tư vấn cá nhân hóa bằng trí tuệ nhân tạo
* Abbreviation: SP26SE04209
The global fragrance industry is rapidly evolving, with consumers increasingly expecting personalized shopping experiences both online and in physical stores. Traditional perfume retail faces several challenges:
•        Limited personalization: Customers often struggle to select fragrances that match their preferences, occasions, or budgets, leading to decision fatigue and abandoned purchases.
•        Inventory inefficiencies: Manual stock management frequently causes overstock, expired products, or stockouts, impacting revenue and customer satisfaction.
•        Fragmented sales channels: Synchronizing in-store and online sales, integrating multiple payment options, and tracking customer loyalty is often cumbersome.
•        Data underutilization: Retailers often have access to large amounts of customer reviews, purchasing patterns, and market trends but lack AI-driven insights to leverage this data effectively.
PerfumeGPT addresses these challenges by combining full retail management with AI-powered perfume consultation. By simulating a fragrance expert using AI APIs, PerfumeGPT provides:
•        Highly personalized fragrance recommendations, increasing customer satisfaction and conversion rates.
•        Automated inventory alerts and import management, reducing stock errors and losses due to expired products.
•        Integrated sales channels, combining in-store POS, e-commerce platforms, and diverse payment methods.
•        Actionable insights from customer reviews, trends, and purchase data, enabling smarter merchandising, marketing, and promotional strategies.
Through these capabilities, PerfumeGPT not only streamlines operational efficiency but also enhances the customer journey, bridging the gap between human-like expertise and digital retail management, ultimately driving sales growth, brand loyalty, and data-driven decision-making.

2. Proposed Solutions 
PerfumeGPT is a comprehensive web and mobile system that integrates an AI-powered perfume consultation engine with complete perfume retail management. It directly addresses current industry challenges by providing:
•        Highly personalized fragrance recommendations through natural-language chatbot and guided quiz
•        Real-time inventory management with low-stock and expiry alerts
•        Unified omnichannel sales (in-store POS + own website + Shopee synchronization)
•        Multiple local payment methods (Momo, VNPay, COD) and shipping partners (GHN, GHTK)
•        AI-driven insights: review summarization, trend forecasting, repurchase suggestions
•        Full CRM with loyalty points, automated promotions, and personalized marketing
•        Analytics dashboard showing revenue, best-sellers, and AI recommendation acceptance rate
 
* Functional requirement 
•  User Account Management
•        SSO login (Google/Facebook)
•        Four roles with strict permissions: Admin, Staff, Customer, AI Consultant
•        Customer profile storing scent preferences, budget range, purchase & quiz history
•  AI Perfume Consultation
•        Natural-language chatbot → returns 3–5 matching products with reasoning
•        5-question interactive quiz (gender, occasion, budget, scent family, longevity) → top 3 ranked recommendations + explanation
•        Advanced matching: scent layer analysis, weather-, age-, style-based suggestions
•  Sales Management
•        In-store POS: barcode scanning, discount/promo application, receipt printing
•        Online store + real-time Shopee order & inventory synchronization
•        Payment gateways: Momo, VNPay, Cash-on-Delivery
•        Shipping integration: GHN, GHTK with automatic status updates
•  AI Integration (xAI API)
•        Natural-language recommendation engine
•        Automatic review summarization across 100+ reviews
•        Sales & scent trend forecasting
•        Semantic product search (e.g., “sweet women’s perfume under 1.5 million VND”)
•  CRM & Promotions
•        Loyalty points (1 point = 1,000 VND spent)
•        Combo deals, free samples, personalized repurchase reminders
•        Automated email/SMS marketing campaigns
•  Notifications
•        Push, email, SMS for order status, promotions, restocks, and new AI recommendations
•  Dashboards & Reporting
•        Admin dashboard: revenue, inventory levels, top products, AI acceptance rate
•        Staff dashboard: today’s orders, quick counter consultation tool
•  Mobile Applications
•        Customer app: AI chat, quiz, browsing, ordering, order tracking
•        Staff app: mobile POS, stock checking, quick customer consultation

*    Non-functional requirement: 
•  Response time: AI consultation < 3 seconds, page load < 2 seconds 
•  Scalability: support multiple stores and peak traffic (cloud-ready) 
•  Reliability: 99.9% uptime, automatic database backup, graceful handling of xAI API downtime 
•  Compatibility: responsive web + iOS/Android apps
